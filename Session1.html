Now lets do a serious bussines that will help us a lot for the next sessions. Lets make a sine time series with a period of 12 hours for a month

In [37]:
t=np.arange(1,30,1/24)#Im creating a vector of time from day 1 to day 30 with a time inteval of 1/24 days (1 hour)
omega=2*np.pi/(12/24)#I define a frequency for my sine function. This concept will be reviewed in the next session
n=np.sin(omega*t)

plt.plot(t,n)
Out[37]:
[<matplotlib.lines.Line2D at 0x22818d6f7c0>]
